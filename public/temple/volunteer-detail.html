About Sai Krishna (Sarthakji)

Sai Krishna (Sarthakji) is a dedicated priest at Shri Shirdi Sai Baba Mandir Chicago. Our mission is to spread the teachings of Shri Sai Baba, promoting love, compassion, and service to humanity. Our temple provides spiritual guidance and conducts various pujas and aartis for devotees. Our priests follow the teachings of Shri Sai Baba, emphasizing love, compassion, and selfless service. We conduct regular bhajans, satsang, and special programs to help devotees deepen their spiritual practice and experience Baba's divine grace.

Shri Shirdi Sai Baba Mandir Chicago offers daily aarti services including Kakad Aarti, Madhyahna Aarti, Dhoop Aarti, and Shej Aarti. Our priests conduct various pujas including Archana, Abhishek, Sri Sai Satya Vrat, and other traditional ceremonies. We welcome devotees to experience the divine presence of Sai Baba and participate in our spiritual programs.

Skills

Sai Krishna holds M.A – Acharya – Aapasthaba Pourohityam (2010-2012) and has studied at Tirumala Tirupati Vedic University. He completed Krishna Yajurvedam and Smartham in Gayatri Shankara Vedapatashala, Hyderabad. He joined Shirdi Sai Baba Mandir, Chicago in November 2022 and is proficient in conducting various pujas, aartis, and religious ceremonies.
