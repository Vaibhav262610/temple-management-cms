Thursday Shej Aarati Mahaprasad — Guidelines
  1. Registration is open on first come first serve basis.
  2. Food must be freshly cooked (no overnight cooking).
  3. Kindly make sure not to include garlic.
  4. Bring food in clean Silver Foil trays and keep covered. Do not bring prasad in personal containers.

Sri Sai Deva Palki Yatra
https://saisamsthanusa.org/sri-sai-deva-palki-yatra/

Sri Sai Deva Palki Yatra
INVITE BABA IN PALKI TO YOUR HOME
What is Sri Sai Deva Palki Yatra? — ceremonial travel of Sri Sai Baba in the Palki.
Why is this organized? — To invoke Lord Sainath’s grace and bring peace and happiness to devotees and the community.
How to participate — as Local Coordinator, Host, or Volunteer.
Local Coordinator — coordinates at least five homes; registers; works with Mandir Palkhi coordinator; schedule is approved; flyer/schedule is created and publicized; accompanies Palki through hosts until return for Dhoop Aarati.
Host — invites Palki to their home; can work with coordinator to be one of the five homes.
Volunteer — can join at Mandir or at hosts’ homes; may drive, distribute flyers/posters.
At host home — welcomes with Purna Kumbham; Punyaha Vachanam; Astotra & Aarati; Prasad distribution.
Pre/Post events — decorate Palki at Mandir; clean‑up post‑Yatra.
Fees — none; donations voluntary and tax‑deductible.
Timing — can occur any time; communities beyond 50 miles should contact Mandir to plan.
